Akshardham temple is new temple but denote Indian history, architecture and Hindu culture. Akshardham temple is also known as Swaminarayan Akshardham or Delhi Akshardham temple

Architecture

Akshardham temple Structure shows a great Indian culture and its architecture is simply impeccable.
This amazing property consists of 20,000 statues, floral motifs and exquisite carved stone pillars. This beautiful structure and an architectural masterpiece built in the support framework and financing Bochasanvasi Aksharpurushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha (PAB). Akshar dham temple is built using guidelines of Vastu Shastra and Pancharatra Shastra.

Stones

Akshardham temple does not have any steel in building. It is made of pink stones and white marbles
Pink stone denotes Hindu Shradha, Bhakti. White color denotes purity.
Temple has of 234 carved pillars, 9 ornate domes, 20 quadrangled shikhars, a Gajendra Pith (plinth of stone elephants) and 20,000 murtis and statues of India’s great sadhus, devotees, acharyas and divine personalities.

It is built on the river Yamuna and the surface area of approximately 100 acres of beautiful gardens, fountains and carved pavilions.

When it was Built

The temple was inaugurated on November 6, 2005 Pramukhswami Maharaj (Chief BAPS). The temple was completed in two years An estimated $ 2 billion were spent for the construction of a masterpiece Hindu temple.

Akshardham temple center has main monument. It is is 141-foot (43 m) high, 316-foot (96 m) wide, and 370-foot (110 m) long.
Main monument is covered top to bottom with carved details of flora, fauna, dancers, musicians, and deities.

The temple is located in NH 24, Noida Mor New Delhi.
